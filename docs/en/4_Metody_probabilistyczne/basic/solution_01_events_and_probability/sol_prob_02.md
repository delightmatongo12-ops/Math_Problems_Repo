

# Task 2 — Rolling a Die

## Sample Space

A fair die has outcomes

$$
\Omega = {1,2,3,4,5,6}
$$

---

## One Roll

$$
\Omega_1 = {1,2,3,4,5,6}
$$

Number of outcomes:

$$
|\Omega_1| = 6
$$

---

## Two Rolls

The sample space is the **Cartesian product**

$$
\Omega_2 = \Omega \times \Omega
$$

Example outcomes:

$$
(1,1),(1,2),(1,3),\dots,(6,6)
$$

Total outcomes:

$$
|\Omega_2| = 6^2 = 36
$$

---

## Three Rolls

$$
\Omega_3 = \Omega^3
$$

Total outcomes:

$$
|\Omega_3| = 6^3 = 216
$$

---

## Example Outcome

$$
(2,5,3)
$$

means

* first roll = 2
* second roll = 5
* third roll = 3

---

## Sample Questions

### Q1: How many outcomes exist for two rolls?

Answer:

$$
|\Omega_2| = 6^2 = 36
$$

---

### Q2: What does the outcome $(4,1,6)$ represent?

Answer:

* first roll = 4
* second roll = 1
* third roll = 6

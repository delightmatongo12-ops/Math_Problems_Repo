# **Task 8 — Sequences with Repetition**

### **Question**

1. 5-digit PIN codes
2. At least one repetition
3. All digits different

---

## **Solutions**

### **1.**

$$10^5 = 100000$$

---

### **2.**

$$10^5 - P(10,5) = 100000 - 30240 = 69760$$

---

### **3.**

$$P(10,5) = 30240$$

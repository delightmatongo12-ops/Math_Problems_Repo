# **Task 4 — Circular Permutations**

### **Question**

1. 7 people around a table
2. Two must sit together
3. Two must sit opposite

---

## **Solutions**

### **1.**

$$(7-1)! = 6! = 720$$

---

### **2.**

Treat pair as block:

$$(6-1)! \cdot 2 = 5! \cdot 2 = 240$$

---

### **3.**

Fix one person → opposite position fixed:

$$5! = 120$$

---

## **Teacher Questions**

* Why $(n-1)!$? → Rotations identical.
* Why fix a person? → Remove symmetry.

# **Task 9 — Digit Restrictions**

### **Question**

1. 5-digit numbers
2. Even numbers
3. No repetition
4. At least one repetition

---

## **Solutions**

### **1.**

$$9 \cdot 10^4 = 90000$$

---

### **2.**

Last digit even (0,2,4,6,8):

$$9 \cdot 10^3 \cdot 5 = 45000$$

---

### **3.**

$$9 \cdot 9 \cdot 8 \cdot 7 \cdot 6 = 27216$$

---

### **4.**

$$90000 - 27216 = 62784$$

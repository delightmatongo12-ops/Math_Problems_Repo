# **Task 5 — Combinations**

### **Question**

1. Choose 4 from 12
2. Contain a particular student
3. At least one of two students
4. Exactly 2 women (7 men, 5 women)

---

## **Solutions**

### **1.**

$$\binom{12}{4} = 495$$

---

### **2.**

$$\binom{11}{3} = 165$$

---

### **3.**

$$\binom{12}{4} - \binom{10}{4} = 495 - 210 = 285$$

---

### **4.**

$$\binom{5}{2}\binom{7}{2} = 10 \cdot 21 = 210$$

---

## **Teacher Questions**

* Why subtract in (3)? → Complement method.
* Why multiply in (4)? → Independent choices.

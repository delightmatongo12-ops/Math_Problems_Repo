# **Task 6 — Card Combinations**

### **Question**

1. Exactly 2 hearts
2. At least one heart
3. No face cards

---

## **Solutions**

### **1.**

$$\binom{13}{2}\binom{39}{3} = 78 \cdot 9139 = 712842$$

---

### **2.**

$$\binom{52}{5} - \binom{39}{5} = 2598960 - 575757 = 2023203$$

---

### **3.**

Non-face cards = 40:

$$\binom{40}{5} = 658008$$

---

## **Teacher Questions**

* Why subtract in (2)? → Easier than direct counting.
* Why 39 cards? → Non-hearts.
